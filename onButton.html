Where is The On Button?

My family refers to me as the “technology help-desk”, and I’m used to going over for a holiday and being asked by various people to help fix their phones or computers. So, when the infamous COVID-19 lockdown began, I was getting even more questions from my relatives on technological issues. Since COVID-19 made lots of things zoom-oriented and people had to use technology more, I decided to make my own website whereistheonbutton.com in order to help everyone understand technology better. The goal of my website is to provide clear, easy steps to follow in order to help people use technology without being overwhelmed. I have a section that specifically describes how to use Zoom and another that helped people make vaccine appointments. I love coding so much and wanted to use my skills to help others. I made this website using html, CSS, and Javascript :)
